This was a competitive project to display working on a real world application with a team. For 2020, there were 6 teams with 5 people on each team. We had 13 weeks to complete the game. At the Halfway, we had a User Interface Presentation to showcase an early prototype and to receive feedback. There were also smaller deadlines that were assigned to show individual progress for everyone. My team used the Game Engine, Unity, to convert the board game, Quarto, into a Computer Game. My team, Kids In A Trenchcoat, finished 4th out of 6 teams. The difference between 3rd and 4th was 1.5 points. We won 1st place in the AI competition for Best AI. Our hardest AI never lost against other AI or human competitors.
Our theme was centered around a potion shop in a fantasy setting. The master of this potion shop invites new challengers to compete against each other to become the master’s apprentice. The fire outside signals challengers where to locate the potion shop. Once inside you must strategize in order to be victorious. Lose and you’ll go home empty handed.
